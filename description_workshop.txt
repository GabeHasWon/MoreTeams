--- More Teams ---
by GabeHasWon
commissioned by August

Adds in 10 more teams in multiplayer: the Gray, Orange, Teal, Rose, Black, Lime, Wood, Aquamarine, Purple and Ivory teams.
They behave the same as vanilla teams.

The amount of added teams is modifiable, from +2 to +10 (in steps of 2).

GitHub: https://github.com/GabeHasWon/MoreTeams
